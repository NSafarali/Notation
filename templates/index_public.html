Notation

A simple web app to make drawings that are easy to embed. Click here for more information and a demo. Made by Evert Heylen, source available on github.

This was made specifically to fill a shortcoming in Notion. Use the /embed command and paste the generated URL. All drawings are visible to anyone who has the URL, but only you can edit them.

Cookie and privacy policy: This service uses cookies and local storage to remember your preferences and authentication info. We only store your emailaddress to authenticate. If you want all your data deleted or obtain a copy, please contact the author.
